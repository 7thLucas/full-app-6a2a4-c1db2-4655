The Foundation

The Problem

Deals slip silently. Follow-ups get forgotten, "last touch" is invisible, and there's no single place showing which relationships are going cold. The cost isn't messiness — it's lost revenue from contacts who were never followed up.

The Solution

Connected pieces — contacts, an interaction log, and a pipeline — that keep each other honest, fronted by a Today view that surfaces who's gone cold. Logging a call updates "last touch"; the pipeline always reflects reality. The cold ones become impossible to miss.
